No Bullshit Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

  • 12 oz Lasagna Noodles
  • 1/2 Stick Butter
  • 1 qt Spaghetti Sauce
  • 8 oz Cheddar Cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F.
  2. Brown ground beef, drain, set aside
  3. Layer Lasagna then add 1/4th of pasta sauce, 1/3rd ground beef, 1/3rd cheese
  4. Repeat last step x3
  5. Add butter, leftover sauce, and cheese to top
  6. Bake for 40 minutes

It's just noodles, meat, sauce, and cheese in a dish. It probably won't taste great but it's cheaper than delivery.
